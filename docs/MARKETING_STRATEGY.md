# Marketing Strategy - Laku POS

Target market: Warung, toko kecil, kafe, restoran di Indonesia

---

## Channel Paling Efektif (dari paling → kurang)

### 1. SEO & Content Marketing (Long-term, gratis)

**Why:** Traffic organic terus bertambah, cost per acquisition sangat rendah.

**Action Items:**
- Blog post: "Cara kelola stok warung", "Cara hitung laba rugi", "Tips kasir digital"
- YouTube tutorial: "Cara pakai POS untuk warung makanan"
- Target keyword: "kasir warung", "aplikasi POS gratis", "sistem kasir online"
- Optimize on-page SEO: title tags, meta descriptions, schema markup

**Metrics:**
- Organic traffic growth
- Keyword ranking
- Time on page
- Bounce rate

**Timeline:** 3-6 bulan untuk hasil signifikan

---

### 2. TokoOnline/Shopee/Lazada (B2B2C)

**Why:** Warung yang pakai POS butuh supplier barang. Bisnis jadi reseller POS untuk komisi.

**Action Items:**
- Daftar jadi reseller di platform e-commerce
- Tawarkan bundle: POS + training + support
- Highlight features: offline mode, multi-outlet

**Metrics:**
- Sales from channel
- Commission rate
- Customer acquisition cost

**Timeline:** 1-2 minggu untuk setup

---

### 3. WhatsApp Business & Groups (Paling kuat di Indonesia)

**Why:** Indonesia adalah WhatsApp-first country. Trust tinggi, conversion tinggi.

**Action Items:**
- Join 5-10 grup Facebook "Warung/Kuliner/Minimarket"
- Share demo video dan success stories
- Broadcast ke daftar toko (scrape atau manual outreach)
- Offer: Free 14-day trial, no credit card required

**Script Outreaches:**

> Halo Kak, aku lihat warungnya aktif banget. Aku ada aplikasi kasir digital yang bisa bantu:
> - Pantau stok dari HP
> - Transaksi tetap jalan kalau internet mati
> - Cetak struk otomatis
>
> Free trial 14 hari, nggak perlu kartu kredit. Mau demo gratis via WA?

**Metrics:**
- Response rate
- Conversion rate
- Trial signups
- Paid upgrades

**Timeline:** 1-2 minggu untuk hasil awal

---

### 4. Facebook/Instagram Ads

**Why:** Visual demo works well, bisa target demografi spesifik. Tapi mahal.

**Action Items:**
- Target: Pemilik warung, resto kafe kecil, 25-45 tahun
- Interest: Small business, restaurant, retail, POS, accounting
- Budget start: Rp 100k-300k/hari
- A/B test: Video demo vs Static image carousel

**Creative Framework:**

| Bad Copy | Good Copy |
|-----------|-------------|
| "Download Aplikasi POS Keren" | "Warungmu selalu hitung salah? Pakai ini." |
| "Sistem kasir modern" | "Kasir pulang? Toko tetap bisa jalan." |
| "Aplikasi POS Indonesia" | "Cek stok dari HP, nggak perlu di toko." |

**Pivot Strategy:**
- Run ads untuk "cari kerja kasir" → mereka paksa bos pakai POS
- Ads untuk "buka warung baru" → upsell POS saat startup

**Metrics:**
- CTR (Click-through rate)
- CPC (Cost per click)
- Conversion rate
- CAC (Cost per acquisition)
- ROAS (Return on ad spend)

**Timeline:** 1-2 minggu untuk data awal

---

### 5. Google Ads (PPC)

**Why:** High intent traffic, orang cari solusi spesifik.

**Action Items:**
- Target keyword: "sistem kasir", "aplikasi toko", "pos murah", "kasir digital"
- Local SEO: "kasir digital di [kota]", "POS Jakarta", "POS Surabaya"
- Search ads: Bid on competitor keywords
- Shopping ads: N/A (tidak ada produk fisik)

**Ad Copy Examples:**
> Kasir Digital Warung - Gratis Selamanya. Offline Mode. Multi-Outlet.
>
> Aplikasi POS untuk Toko Kecil. Transaksi Cepat. Stok Real-time.

**Metrics:**
- Quality Score
- CTR
- Conversion rate
- CAC vs LTV ratio

**Timeline:** 1-2 minggu untuk live

---

### 6. Cold Outreach / Direct Sales

**Why:** Tingkat closing tinggi, tapi manual dan skalabilitas terbatas.

**Action Items:**
- Google Maps: Cari warung/cafe di area target
- Buka website/Instagram: Cek info kontak
- Call/WhatsApp: Offer demo langsung
- Demo di tempat: Closing lebih tinggi

**Script:**

> Halo Kak, aku lihat warungnya di Google Maps. Kelihat ramai banget!
>
> Aku bantu pasang sistem kasir digital yang bisa:
> - Bantu hitung penjualan otomatis
> - Alert kalau stok mau habis
> - Export laporan untuk pajak/bukuan
>
> Bisa demo gratis sekarang? 15 menit cukup.

**Metrics:**
- Call volume
- Demo conversion
- Sale closure rate
- Cost per sale (time-based)

**Timeline:** Manual, hasil tergantung effort

## KPI yang Harus Tracking

### Acquisition Metrics
- **CAC** (Cost per User Acquisition): Target < Rp 50k untuk plan Warung (Rp 99rb/mo)
- **MRR Growth** (Monthly Recurring Revenue): Rp 5jt/bulan target awal
- **Trial-to-Paid Conversion**: Target > 10%

### Engagement Metrics
- **DAU/MAU** (Daily/Monthly Active Users)
- **Session Duration**: > 5 menit
- **Feature Usage**: POS aktif, export laporan, stok alerts

### Retention Metrics
- **Churn Rate**: <5%/bulan healthy, >10% concern
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value): Target 6-12 bulan per user
- **NPS** (Net Promoter Score): >40 good

## Creative Best Practices

### Video Content (TikTok/IG Reels)
1. Hook 3 detik: "Kalau kasir pulang..." / "Hitung manual salah..."
2. Show real problem: Paper receipt pile, calculator confusion
3. Show solution: Phone interface, auto sync
4. CTA: "Link di bio, free trial"

### Static Creative (FB/IG)
- Use screenshots of app in Indonesian
- Show pricing clearly (Free plan prominent)
- Use real reviews/testimonials if available
- Testimonial format: Photo + Name + Business type

### Landing Page Optimization
- Headline: Value prop dalam 8 kata
- Social proof: Counter of active users, revenue processed
- Trust signals: Row Level Security mentioned, 100% Indonesian owned
- CTA: "Coba Gratis" - emphasize no credit card
